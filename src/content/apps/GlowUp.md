A PWA designed for beauty clinic receptionists who need practical English for their daily work. Instead of grammar lessons, GlowUp focuses on real situations: greeting clients, managing appointments, giving directions, handling payments, and dealing with complaints. Features daily 5-minute exercises that mix listening comprehension, response selection, and pronunciation practice. Includes a searchable cheat sheet organized by situation for quick reference at work. Tracks streaks with celebratory animations and adapts speech speed as the user levels up.
