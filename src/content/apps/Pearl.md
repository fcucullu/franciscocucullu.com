Pearl is a native mobile period and cycle tracker that learns your unique rhythm. Log your periods and Pearl predicts your cycle, showing four phases (menstrual, follicular, ovulation, luteal) on a beautiful color-coded calendar. Each phase comes with personalized recommendations on how you might feel and what to do. Track daily symptoms like mood, energy, and pain. Get native push notifications for phase changes and set up partner notifications to automatically email your partner with tips on how to support you. Available on Google Play and as a PWA.
