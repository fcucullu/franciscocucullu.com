Clientísima is a client management platform designed for women entrepreneurs running service businesses, beauty salons, wellness centers, clinics, and more. It covers everything from client relationships and appointment scheduling to payments, loyalty programs, and service catalogs. Supports multiple professionals with individual Google Calendar integration. Built as a webapp optimized for desktop and tablet use in-store. Features include a chatbot, automated reminders, and detailed client history tracking.
