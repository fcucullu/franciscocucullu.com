Scout is a focused AI product that runs a personalized job screening every morning. You upload your CV and tell Scout the roles you're after; each day an AI sweeps the market and hands you a curated shortlist of real, verified, currently-open roles that match your profile — scored for fit, with no noise and no expired posts. It arrives in a web dashboard and, optionally, your inbox. Scout finds and filters the right roles for you; you stay in control.

The reason Scout exists is a product thesis: a generic research agent is a capability, not a product. Scout wraps that capability in the things that make it actually useful — a persistent profile grounded in your CV, daily verified screening, fit scoring, and a roles tracker — so the user gets a dependable outcome instead of a clever demo. Built as a focused product on top of a general LLM.
