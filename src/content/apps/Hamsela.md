Hamsela unifies everything a mentor, coach or therapist needs to accompany their clients, replacing the patchwork of separate apps. Each client gets their own private space (an installable PWA) showing their sessions and summaries, tasks and commitments with due dates, a milestone-based progress journey, and shared resources. Mentors get a Clientísima-style web admin: invite students, run cohorts, log sessions with private internal notes, and see how the whole group is progressing in a visual dashboard. Built multi-tenant from day one with strict row-level security so it can be sold to other mentors, with tiered plans (free up to 20 students, then paid tiers) billed via Stripe. Phase 2 adds an AI assistant that answers in the mentor's own voice between sessions.
