A mobile-first Progressive Web App for tracking recurring events and spotting patterns in your daily life. Create custom events with emoji and colors, then log occurrences with a satisfying double-tap and confetti animation. The dashboard shows frequency charts across 24 hours, 7 days, or 30 days, with detailed stats per event. Share events with other users for collaborative tracking — perfect for monitoring things like pet health, habits, or symptoms together with family members. Built with Google OAuth for quick sign-in and designed as a PWA for a native app-like experience.
