A mobile-first Progressive Web App that helps you maintain meaningful relationships. Life gets busy and we often lose touch with important people simply because there's no system in place. Golden List solves that by letting you set customizable reminder frequencies for each contact — weekly, biweekly, monthly, or any custom interval. It tracks your full interaction history, sends push notifications when it's time to reach out, and gives you a clean dashboard that surfaces who needs your attention right now. Built with Google OAuth for quick sign-in and designed as a PWA for a native app-like experience — no app store required.
