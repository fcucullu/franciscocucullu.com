Doodl is an intimate, playful PWA for two people to communicate through drawings. Create a room and share a pairing code — no account needed. Draw on a full-screen canvas with multiple colors, brush sizes, and an eraser, then send your doodle. Your partner sees it instantly via Supabase real-time subscriptions and can react with emojis. The feed shows all exchanged doodles chronologically, making it a visual conversation thread.
