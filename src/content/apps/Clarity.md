Clarity solves the gap between setting goals and actually working toward them daily. It combines goal manifesting with a structured journaling practice — you set goals with target dates and categories (health, career, relationships, personal, financial, spiritual), then journal with intention by linking entries to specific goals. The app tracks your writing streak, mood patterns over time, and offers daily prompts to keep you consistent. Built with Google OAuth for easy sign-in, it works as a PWA that you can install on any device.
