Sun Tracker is a mobile-first PWA that shows the sun's exact position in real-time, overlaid on your phone's camera feed. Point your phone at the sky and see a glowing indicator where the sun actually is. A dotted trajectory line shows the full sun path from sunrise to sunset. Built as a single HTML file with no backend — pure browser APIs for geolocation, camera, and device orientation.
