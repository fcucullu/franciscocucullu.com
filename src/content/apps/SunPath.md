SunPath is a mobile-first PWA that shows the sun's daily path across the sky on a beautiful compass diagram. See exactly where the sun rises and sets, browse past and future days, compare day lengths, and find golden hour times. Built as a single HTML file with no backend — pure browser APIs for geolocation and compass orientation.
