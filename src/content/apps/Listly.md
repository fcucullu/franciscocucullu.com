Listly is a PWA for sharing shopping lists with the people you care about. Create multiple lists (groceries, wish list, etc.), share them with specific people by email, and collaborate in real-time via Supabase subscriptions. Checking off items triggers a satisfying confetti animation. Built with Google OAuth, emerald green theme, and designed as a mobile-first PWA.
