Listly is a collaborative task list app for couples, roommates, and small teams, available as a native Android app on Google Play and as a mobile-first PWA. Create multiple lists, share them by email, assign items to specific members with colored initials, and set a draggable "today" line to prioritize what matters now. Real-time sync via Supabase means everyone sees changes instantly. Built with Google OAuth and an emerald green theme.
