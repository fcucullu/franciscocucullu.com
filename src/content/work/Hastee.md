## Business Case

Hastee was an advanced wages app that let employees access part of their salary before payday, along with other employee perks. When I joined, engineering delivery was slow and misaligned with business priorities.

## Impact

- **Increased engineering output by 137%** by redesigning delivery processes
- **Led 13 engineers across 3 technical teams**
- **Implemented prioritization framework** aligning roadmap with C-level objectives
- **Improved resource allocation** across all 3 teams
- **Integrated with multiple ERP systems** across the EU to enable the product at scale

## Role

Tech Product Manager for 1.5 years. Owned the operational transformation of engineering and product delivery.
