## Business Case

FlexRent was a room exchange platform for digital nomads, like HomeExchange but for a different niche. I identified the market opportunity, built the product end-to-end, and grew the community from zero.

## Impact

- **620 community members** built from scratch
- **32 paying customers** (completed exchanges)
- **Pitched at South Summit** (Europe's leading startup conference)
- **Pitched at Warsaw Startup Stage** (international competition)
- **Pitched at DES Malaga** (Digital Enterprise Show, investors)
- **Featured on TV** (101 TV, Hola Andalucia)
- **Radio interview** on technology and entrepreneurship
- **Secured angel investment**
- **Led a team of up to 9** across product and engineering

## Role

Founder & Product Lead for 10 months. Designed and shipped the MVP end-to-end (product, backend, frontend, analytics). Led customer discovery and iterative feature validation.
