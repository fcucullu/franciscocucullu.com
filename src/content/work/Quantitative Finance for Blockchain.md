## Business Case

Xcapit was a blockchain-based fintech startup focused on making financial services accessible through AI and DeFi. We combined algorithmic trading with financial inclusion to serve underbanked communities.

## Impact

- **$2M portfolio managed** with custom algorithmic trading strategies
- **400%+ annual returns** through AI-driven portfolio optimization
- **$650K in microloans** delivered to 330 underbanked individuals in Argentina
- **AI Innovation Award** for integrating AI/ML into blockchain wealth management
- **Recognized as a [Digital Public Good](https://app.digitalpublicgoods.net/a/11545)**
- **Funded by UNICEF Innovation Fund and Ethereum Foundation**
- **Featured in [Cronista](https://www.cronista.com/infotechnology/finanzas-digitales/la-unica-inversion-que-hoy-paga-400-en-dolares-sin-riesgo-argentino-ni-impuestos/), [CriptoNoticias](https://www.criptonoticias.com/comunidad/ceo-xcapit-defi-democraticas-porque-barreras-mas-bajas/), and [Corporate Vision](https://www.corporatevision-news.com/winners/xcapit/)**
- **Team:** 5 PhDs (mathematics, physics, computer science)

## What We Built

1. **Algorithmic trading engine** with custom genetic algorithms and LSTM models
2. **AI portfolio management** as a service for retail users
3. **Guarantee provider platform** enabling microloans for financially excluded individuals
4. **Open-source crypto wallet** recognized by UNICEF
