## Business Case

The company wanted to guarantee the best FX price in the market. Marketing was running a campaign promising "the best price" but had no way to verify it in real-time. C-level executives asked me to solve this.

## Impact

- **21 scrapers covering 100% of competitors** in the Argentine FX market
- **Captured 20% of the target market** by enabling a data-driven pricing strategy
- **Built mobile app emulators** for 2 competitors that had no public website
- **Flask monitoring dashboard** for the team to track prices across the market
- **Solo project:** designed, built, and maintained entirely by myself

## Technical Details

- Python with Selenium and BeautifulSoup for web scraping
- Mobile device emulation for app-only competitors
- Real-time price aggregation and comparison
- Flask web interface for the pricing team
- Scheduled automation for continuous monitoring
