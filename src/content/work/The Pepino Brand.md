## Business Case

The Pepino Brand was a B2B SaaS platform helping branding agencies manage all their digital assets (Digital Asset Manager). I co-founded the company and built the entire product and technical infrastructure from zero.

## Impact

- **Rebuilt the product from scratch** with scalable architecture
- **Closed 7 recurring B2B customers** (marketing agencies)
- **Landed major clients** including Credo and BBK Foundation
- **Led a team of 3** (2 engineers + myself)
- **Defined product vision, go-to-market, and monetization strategy**
- **Secured angel investment** during early-stage validation

## Role

CPTO & Co-founder for 1.5 years. Owned product, engineering, and discovery cycles.
