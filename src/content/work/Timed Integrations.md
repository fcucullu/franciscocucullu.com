## Business Case

Factorial's HR platform had a gap: clients using Italian ERP systems had to manually input time tracking data. I identified this opportunity, built an integration layer that automated the data ingestion, and sold the product to Factorial.

## Impact

- **Built and sold a micro-SaaS** to Factorial (HR unicorn)
- **Automated time tracking ingestion** from Italian ERPs into Factorial
- **Eliminated manual data entry** for Factorial's Italian market clients
- **Full 0→1 execution:** identified opportunity, built product, closed acquisition

## Role

Solo founder. Designed, developed, and sold the product.
