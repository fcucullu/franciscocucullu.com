## Business Case

After market close, the 5-person trading desk at IOL spent hours manually checking balances, verifying operations, and reconciling data across systems. I automated the entire post-market administrative process.

## Impact

- **Saved 5+ hours daily** (1 hour per team member, 5 people)
- **Reduced post-market admin time to zero** with fully automated reconciliation
- **Freed the team** to transition from operations to Business Intelligence
- **I eventually led the newly formed BI team** that emerged from this transformation
- **Team:** 5 people in the trading desk

## Evolution

1. **VBA Macros:** Started by automating Excel/Access data processing
2. **Python ETL:** Combined multiple data sources with scrapers and scheduled jobs
3. **Full Automation:** End-to-end pipeline covering navigation, extraction, transformation, export, and scheduling
