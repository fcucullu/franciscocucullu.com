## Business Case

As the data lead at Xcapit, every stakeholder in the company came to me with data questions. Product, marketing, operations, investors. The constant interruptions were killing my productivity and creating bottlenecks for the whole organization.

## Impact

- **Reduced ad-hoc data requests to 0%** by making data self-serve
- **Full company transparency:** anyone could access data anytime
- **Democratized analytics** across all departments (product, marketing, ops)
- **Freed my time completely** to focus on building ML models instead of answering queries

## How It Works

Team members send natural language commands in Discord. The bot interprets the request, queries the database, formats the response, and returns structured data instantly. Available 24/7 to the entire company.
